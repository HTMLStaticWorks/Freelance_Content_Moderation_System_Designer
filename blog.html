Frequently Asked Questions

Common inquiries regarding trust & safety infrastructure and strategy.

How often should we audit our moderation taxonomy?

We recommend a full deep-dive audit every 6 months, or immediately following a major platform policy change. Regional modifiers should be reviewed quarterly to ensure they remain aligned with local legal standards.

What is the ideal ratio between AI and human review?

There is no "ideal" ratio, but high-performing systems typically automate 95%+ of low-risk content, reserving human review for high-severity, high-ambiguity, or appealed decisions. The goal is to maximize AI efficiency without sacrificing accuracy.

How do you handle secondary trauma for human reviewers?

Our infrastructure designs prioritize "Reviewer Ergonomics." This includes grayscale viewing modes, automated blurring of graphic content, strictly enforced shift rotations, and integrated wellness tracking within the moderation dashboard.
