Solutions I Offer

For social media, marketplaces, gaming platforms, and online communities.

Rule Engine Design

Your platform has policies — but are they actually enforced consistently? I translate community guidelines into precise, machine-executable logic that scales without drift.

Queue Architecture

When harmful content sits in a backlog for hours, real people get hurt. I design priority routing systems that get the most critical content reviewed first — fast.

False Positive Auditing

Over-aggressive filtering frustrates good users and drives churn. I audit your detection thresholds and fix the imbalance between safety and user experience.

AI + Human Workflows

AI catches volume, humans handle nuance. I design the handoff between automated detection and human review so nothing sensitive falls through the cracks.

Policy Documentation

Inconsistent reviewer decisions start with unclear policies. I build violation taxonomies and decision trees your entire global team can actually use.

Escalation Design

Some content can't wait. I build fail-safe escalation paths for high-severity events — CSAM, credible threats, election interference — so the right person sees it immediately.
