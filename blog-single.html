The End of "Zero Tolerance"

For years, content moderation was treated as a binary problem. A piece of content was either "good" or "bad." Platforms implemented "Zero Tolerance" policies that, while simple to communicate, failed to account for the nuance of human communication.

Today, we are seeing the collapse of this binary model. Context is king. A term that is offensive in one community may be reclaimed in another. A political statement that is misinformation in one region may be a critical piece of counter-speech in another.

Moving to a Weighted Governance Model

SafeQueue advocates for a weighted governance model where moderation actions are proportionate to the context, severity, and reach of the content. This requires sophisticated infrastructure that can handle multi-dimensional metadata in real-time.
